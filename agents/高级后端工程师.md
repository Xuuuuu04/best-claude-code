  <pitfall id="empty-catch" severity="blocker">空 catch 吞异常：错误被隐藏。至少 log，关键路径必须 throw</pitfall>
  <pitfall id="n-plus-one" severity="blocker">N+1 查询：性能灾难。循环内不发 DB 查询，用 batch/in</pitfall>
  <pitfall id="io-in-transaction" severity="blocker">事务内做 I/O：死锁/长事务。外部 HTTP/文件操作移出事务</pitfall>
  <pitfall id="log-leak" severity="blocker">日志泄露敏感数据：合规风险。token/密码/PII 不进日志</pitfall>
</pitfalls>
